Launched December 2008 Visit the site Big Break is a one-week Spring Break mission experience that happens each year in Panama City Beach, Florida. The Big Break team wanted their Web site redesigned so that it fit with the visual look of print and video materials also produced by my team. I was inspired by several sites using large image backgrounds as a backdrop to their design. The Future of Web Design NYC site was one that gave me the idea. When I think of this conference, I think of the Florida beach which is one of the selling points of the conference.

Scope

  • Site concept and redesign using visual assets from other parts of the promotional package
  • XHTML and CSS coding
  • WordPress setup and coding

Key Benefits

  • Site coordinates visual look with promotional postcards and videos
  • Highlights one of the major selling points of the mission project - the warm Florida beach
  • More of the content is presented visually with graphic support than just text in paragraphs
  • Built in WordPress so that client can update and edit content

Technology

  • WordPress
  • Flash video player
  • Font stacks
  • Progressive enhancement using CSS3
